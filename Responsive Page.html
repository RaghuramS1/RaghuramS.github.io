About

Karpagam Academy of Higher Education (KAHE), Coimbatore is Deemed to be a university established under Section 3 of the UGC Act, 1956. Karpagam Academy of Higher Education (KAHE) was established in the year 2008 by The Karpagam Charity Trust. Karpagam Academy of Higher Education (KAHE), deemed to be a university in Coimbatore, located at Eachanari Post, Coimbatore. This 25 Acres sized campus is approved by the Ministry of Human Resource Development, Government of India, Approved by UGC-AICTE, New Delhi, Approved by Council of Architecture, New Delhi, Approved by the Pharmacy Council of India, New Delhi. This college is recognized by NAAC

Features

Karpagam University was established under Section 3 of the UGC Act 1956, of University Grants Commission, Government of India, New Delhi. Recognized by the Ministry of Human Resource Development, Department of Higher Education, Government of India, New Delhi. Accredited with A+ grade by NAAC in the Second Cycle Recognized by UGC-AICTE to conduct Engineering/Technology Programs Recognized by CoA to offer Architecture Programs Recognized by PCI to offer Pharmacy Programs
